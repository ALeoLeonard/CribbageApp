## Description (4000 chars max)

Play the classic card game of cribbage against three tiers of intelligent AI — or pass the device and play with a friend.

Whether you're a seasoned player sharpening your strategy or learning the game for the first time, Cribbage delivers a beautiful, full-featured experience designed for players who love the game.

THREE AI OPPONENTS
• Easy AI picks cards at random — perfect for casual play and learning.
• Medium AI samples strong plays — a satisfying challenge for most players.
• Hard AI evaluates every possible discard and play using full expected-value analysis — the toughest opponent you'll find on the App Store.

SMART HINTS
Stuck on a discard? Tap the hint button for advice powered by the same engine as Hard AI. Every suggestion comes with a score breakdown so you understand why it's the best play.

INTERACTIVE TUTORIAL
Never played cribbage before? The guided tutorial walks you through dealing, discarding, pegging, and counting — right on the game board with real cards. Plus a detailed How to Play reference you can revisit anytime.

DEEP CUSTOMIZATION
Make the game yours with 30 cosmetic items across 8 categories:
• Card backs and card fronts — change the look of every card in play.
• Table and board themes — from classic green felt to modern minimalism.
• Peg styles — brass, ivory, ruby, jade, obsidian, and more.
• Sound packs — swap the entire audio palette.
• Haptic packs — choose how the game feels in your hands.
• Phrase packs — change the commentator's personality from classic calls to grandpa wisdom to trash talk.

GAME CENTER
Compete on three leaderboards (win streak, highest hand, total wins) and unlock 13 achievements — from your first victory to the legendary 29-point perfect hand.

PASS-AND-PLAY
Hand the device to a friend for local two-player games with hidden-hand privacy.

COMPLETE CRIBBAGE RULES
• Muggins rule (optional) — claim missed points from your opponent.
• Skunk and double-skunk tracking.
• Nobs, his heels, and all standard scoring.

STATS & PROGRESS
Track wins, losses, streaks, average hand scores, and more. Your stats sync across devices with iCloud.

PREMIUM UPGRADE — ONE PRICE, NO SUBSCRIPTIONS
The full game is free to play with two AI difficulties and free cosmetic items. Unlock everything for a single $4.99 purchase:
• Hard AI difficulty
• All premium themes, card styles, peg styles, sound packs, haptic packs, and phrase packs
• No ads, ever

PRIVACY FIRST
No accounts. No personal data collected. Analytics are fully anonymous and privacy-friendly. Read our in-app privacy policy anytime from Settings.
