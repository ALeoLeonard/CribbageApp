# Apple Game Center Integration

**Goal**: Leverage Apple's free platform infrastructure for leaderboards, achievements, and social sharing to increase discoverability and retention.

**Success criteria**:
- Game Center authenticates on launch (non-blocking)
- Leaderboards for win streak, highest hand score, and win rate by difficulty
- 12-15 achievements covering milestones from beginner to expert
- Players can share game results as images
