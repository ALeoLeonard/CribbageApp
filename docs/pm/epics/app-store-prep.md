# App Store Launch Readiness

**Goal**: Prepare all non-code assets and infrastructure needed for App Store submission and launch.

**Success criteria**:
- Analytics and crash reporting active in production build
- App icon designed for all required sizes
- App Store screenshots for iPhone (6.7" and 6.1") and iPad (12.9")
- Store listing optimized with title, subtitle, description, and keywords
- Privacy policy published and linked

## Tickets
- [ ] ticket-analytics-setup: Firebase/TelemetryDeck analytics + crash reporting
- [ ] ticket-app-icons: App icon design (all sizes)
- [ ] ticket-screenshots: App Store screenshots (iPhone + iPad)
- [ ] ticket-store-listing: Title, subtitle, description, keywords, categories
- [ ] ticket-privacy-policy: Privacy policy page
